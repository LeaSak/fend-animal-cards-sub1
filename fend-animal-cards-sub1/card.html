Whales

The whale's closest living relative is the hippopotamus.

  • The Largest Whale: Blue Whales
  • The Deepest Diver: Cuvier beaked Whales
  • The Loudest Whale: Beluga whales
  • The Biggest Brain: Sperm Whales

There are many different varieties of whales. They are placental marine mammals which prefer the colder waters of the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. Whales evolved from land-living mammals, which is why they have to breathe air regularly through their blowholes, located on top of their head.
